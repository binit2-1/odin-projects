Crew Members

Monkey D. Luffy is the main protagonist of the series and the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates.

Roronoa Zoro is the swordsman of the Straw Hat Pirates and one of Luffy's closest friends.

Sanji is the cook of the crew and one of Luffy's commanders.

Usopp is the sniper of the crew and a talented inventor.

"I don't want to conquer anything. I just think the guy with the most freedom in this whole ocean... is the King of the Pirates!"

- Monkey D. Luffy
